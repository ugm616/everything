Everything Loader
---
**Skill tree for styles of play**
* Clicking Button - more % per click depending on level.
* Holding Button - more % per hold depending on level.
* Combi-Button - bonesus for clicks per second.
* Multi-Button - Allowing the player to open up second and third skill tree.

**Upgrades**
* Upgrades will have there own skill trees
* Single Purchase - Buy once, Higher cost but better skill.
* Multi Purchase - Buy Repeatedly, cheaper cost, not as effective.
* Speed Upgrades - Used to speed up the Loading Bar.
* Power Upgrades - Used to drop more Essence per Loading Bar completion.
* Player will choose on the skill tree "Power" or "Speed". After choosing one, they will be able to unlock the other for 100 Echoes.

**Particles**
* Combine to marge and remove.
* Every 5 removed gives 1 Essence.
* Can have Multi Purchase upgrades.

**Mini-Games (Like "Particles")**
* more mini-games hidden in plain sight.
* From Era's 1-3, the game will be "Particles".
* Era 4 will be "Hunting", in which a small tooth-like shape will appear on the screen for a few seconds. it will appear randomly on the screen once every 20 cycles at a random time. Clicking on it will give the player 5 times the progress speed for 10 seconds (rounded up the the nearest whole number).

**Reboot Reality**
* only thing saved from previous reboot are Echoes and any Meta Upgrades (Upgrades that are bought with Echoes).

**Eras**
* Each era will have 20 Single Purchase Upgrades (10 for Speed, 10 for Power) and 50 Multi Purchase Upgrades (25 for Speed, 25 for Power).
* If the player Chooses Speed at the start of the game, they have to unlock Power for 100 Echoes.
* If the player Chooses Power at the start of the game, they have to unlock Speed for 100 Echoes.
* 1st choice upgrades cost the normal amount (So if the player chooses Speed, that is their 1st choice. if they choose Power, then that is there 1st choice).
* 2nd choice upgrades cost 150% of the normal amount (So if a player chooses Power, then Speed will be their 2nd choice).
